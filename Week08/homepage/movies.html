My Top 5 Movies

#1: Avengers: Endgame

"Avengers: Endgame" is a superhero film released in 2019. The movie follows the aftermath of the devastating events of "Avengers: Infinity War." The remaining Avengers, along with their allies, work together to undo the actions of the villain Thanos and restore balance to the universe. It's a thrilling and emotional conclusion to the Marvel Cinematic Universe's Infinity Saga.

#2: Avengers: Infinity War

"Avengers: Infinity War" is a 2018 superhero film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. In this movie, the Avengers and their allies must come together to stop the powerful and ruthless Thanos from collecting all six Infinity Stones. Thanos aims to use these stones to achieve his goal of wiping out half of all life in the universe. The film features epic battles and emotional moments as the heroes try to prevent Thanos from carrying out his devastating plan. It's a pivotal chapter in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

#3: Spider-Man: Across the Spider Verse

Miles Morales catapults across the Multiverse, where he encounters a team of Spider-People charged with protecting its very existence. When the heroes clash on how to handle a new threat, Miles must redefine what it means to be a hero.

#4: Guardians of the Galaxy Vol.3

Still reeling from the loss of Gamora, Peter Quill rallies his team to defend the universe and one of their own - a mission that could mean the end of the Guardians if not successful.

#5: Fantastic Mr. Fox

(One of my most comfiest movie) An urbane fox cannot resist returning to his farm raiding ways and then must help his community survive the farmers' retaliation.
